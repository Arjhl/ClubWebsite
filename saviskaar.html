SAVISKAAR

Saviskaar is a techno- cultural fest conducted exclusively for first year students of the college by the Malnad Techincal Club. Over the years, it has been instrumental in discovering the phenomenal talent among students and has set the stage for a spectacular beginning to their journey in the college. The fest has hosted cultural events like dance, music, acting, literary events like quiz, debate, poetry and art events like painting, face painting, pencil sketching. The event has successfully helped students to enjoy competition,embrace team work and truly unleash the talent within.
